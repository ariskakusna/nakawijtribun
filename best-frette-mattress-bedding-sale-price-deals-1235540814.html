For the amount of time we spend sleeping each night, isn’t it also time we invested in a good mattress? That’s the philosophy behind Frette, who luxurious mattresses are rooted in a heritage defined by Italian craftsmanship, comfort and care.

Perhaps best known as a purveyor of fine bedding and linens, Frette also makes some of the world’s best mattresses, with technically-advanced innersprings and fine materials like natural cotton and horse hair, which work together to create a more gentle and peaceful sleep.

Regularly up to $27,000, Frette’s mattresses are on sale from $6,400 right now as part of Bloomingdale’s Black Friday sale. It’s a rare chance to score a discount on these high-end mattresses, which are found in many celebrity homes and top hotels but almost never go on sale.

Frette uses only the finest materials for its linens, and the same thought goes into its mattresses, with a breathable cotton layer that keeps you cool while you sleep and a durable horse hair layer that helps to wick away moisture and direct heat away from the body.

FAQs

Is there a Parade free shipping code?

Whilst there isn't a Parade free shipping code, you can get Parade free shipping on orders of $70 or over. Your order should be processed within 1-2 working days and shipped 3-5 working days after this. For orders of $19.99 or less, there is a $10 shipping fee. If your order is over $19.99, then shipping costs $5.

Can I shop the Parade sale?

Usually, Parade runs Black Friday and Cyber Monday sales. These sales have previously helped shoppers save on bestselling underwear at incredibly affordable prices. Last year, Black Friday sales at Parade gave 30% off all orders and 50% off selected orders for newsletter subscribers. Make sure to sign up for the newsletter so you don’t miss out on Black Friday sales. Outside of the sales, you can also save on your favorite products with a Parade discount code. 

Can I get a student discount at Parade?

Yes. Students can save 15% off their online orders at Parade. Simply log into your Student Beans account to verify your student ID. You should then be able to see your unique Parade underwear coupon code. For more information, go to the ‘Student Discount’ page on the Parade website. 

Can I sign up for the Parade email newsletter?

Of course - simply enter your email address into the email pop-up box to receive the Parade email newsletter. It's a great way to stay up to date about the latest news, exclusive offers, sale events, ways to save, and more. 

Does Parade offer returns?

Yes - Parade is happy to refund customers the full total of their amount within 30 days of the date of purchase. Returns are completely free when completed via the Return Bar.

Previous Instagram posts have included up to 40% off sitewide and $50 gift card giveaways.

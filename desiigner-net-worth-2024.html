Desiigner’s Early Life

Desiigner was born in Brooklyn, New York, and was exposed to music at a young age. His grandfather, Sidney Selby, was a blues musician, and this greatly influenced Desiigner’s interest in music. He began singing in school choirs and church, and at the age of 14, he was shot and decided to focus on his music career.

Rise to Fame

Desiigner’s rise to fame began when he released his debut single “Panda” on Soundcloud and YouTube in 2015. The song quickly gained popularity and caught the attention of rapper Kanye West, who sampled “Panda” in his album “The Life of Pablo”. This led to Desiigner signing with West’s record label, GOOD Music.

Music Career

After signing with GOOD Music, Desiigner released his debut mixtape “New English” in 2016. He also featured on several tracks in Kanye West’s album “The Life of Pablo”. His second single “Timmy Turner” was also a commercial success, further cementing his place in the music industry.

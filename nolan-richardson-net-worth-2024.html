Who is Nolan Richardson?

Nolan Richardson is a former American basketball head coach, best known for his time at the University of Arkansas, where he led the Razorbacks to several NCAA tournaments and a national championship in 1994. His coaching style, known as “40 Minutes of Hell,” was renowned for its intensity and effectiveness.

Solange Knowles Channels Diana Ross

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  102 words  |  Kelle Repass

Media Platforms Design Team

Tonight, Solange Knowles hit the red carpet at the annual CFDA Awards looking almost identical to '70s disco diva Diana Ross. Knowles was dressed for the evening in a metallic body-skimming gown by Francisco Costa for Calvin Klein Collection, which she paired with teased tresses and a major pink lip. She wasn't the only one going for a retro moment—Rihanna made quite the entrance in a near-nude dress and fur stole a la Josephine Baker. Click here to see the best looks of the night.

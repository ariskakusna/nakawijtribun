I have not had a girlfriend for a long time - Falz says as he talks about his love life (video)

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  439 words  |  Billy Koelling

Nigerian rapper and comedian Falz has shared some details with fans about his love life. The rapper talked about marriage, love, and his past relationships.

The entertainer Folarin Falana, known by his stage name Falz, also talked about his rise to fame and the struggles he had to face before his talent was accepted by majority of Nigerians.

Falz had made the revelations during a sit-down with Teni on Flytime Promotion's The Journey. The two celebrities interviewed each other, asking personal questions and talking about their journey to fame.

It is no news that many have tried to find out if Falz is in any kind of relationship, and now he has finally answered the burning question in the hearts of many of his fans. The rapper revealed that he has not had a girlfriend in a long time.

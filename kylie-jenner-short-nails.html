    Kylie Jenner is entering a new era, and we are totally here for it.

    Her new romance with actor and fashion darling Timothée Chalamet has rarely been out of the headlines, prompting fans to analyse Kylie's looks since they went public, eager to see how her style has changed. (Like how older sister Kourt has gone super glam-goth since Travis Barker.)

    One thing that everyone seems to have picked up on is how Kylie's beauty habits have U-turned.

    The reality star is usually devoted to a maximalist manicure, favouring a claw-like, long nail length.

    But switching things up, the 26-year-old traded in her typical look, instead rocking a more understated nail length.

    In a series of stylish snaps shared on Instagram, Kylie showed off her mani moment to her 399m followers.

    Clearly Kylie is embracing a more low-key aesthetic, although, rest assured, she isn't vetoing vibrant shades.

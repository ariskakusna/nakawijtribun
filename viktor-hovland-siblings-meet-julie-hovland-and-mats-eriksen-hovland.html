Who is Viktor Hovland?

Before we dive into his family life, let’s first introduce Viktor Hovland to those who may not be familiar.

Viktor Hovland is a Norwegian professional golfer who gained recognition for his impressive amateur career, including a win at the 2018 U.S. Amateur.

He turned pro in 2019 and quickly made a name for himself on the PGA Tour with several top-10 finishes and a win at the 2020 Puerto Rico Open.

Viktor Hovland Siblings: Meet Julie Hovland and Mats Eriksen Hovland

The two siblings of Viktor Hovland are Julie Hovland and Mats Eriksen Hovland. Not much is known about them.

From naked pictures of Gisele to divorce memes... has Antonio Brown's trolling of Tom Brady gone too

 Posted on August 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  330 words  |  Billy Koelling

Antonio Brown has relentlessly been taking shots at Tom Brady, above all since his divorce with Gisele Bundchen became public.

His latest dig, which is a story of him supposedly sharing a bed with Gisele Bundchen- albeit fully clothed, has been labelled by some as 'disturbing' and a step too far. The post has sent social media into meltdown and has sparked all kinds of rumors.

His merciless mocking of his former friend and teammate is incredibly one sided and difficult to comprehend, especially given Brady has given him no reason to behave in such a way.

Brown's motives

The most bizarre thing about this episode is that no one really knows why Brown is so intent on ridiculing the Tampa quarterback. In fact, from the outside, it appears that Brady has shown nothing but support to his former teammate. According to various sources, the 45-year-old even let Brown temporarily stay at his and Gisele's family home when the receiver moved to Tampa Bay after he was involved in a sexual assault allegation, which forced him make the move away from New England. The pair were two key figures in the Tampa side that went on to win the Superbowl and many praised Brady for helping Brown turn his life around.

A new low for Brown

But since then, things have just gone downhill for Brown. He's currently a free agent and is even wanted by the police , and seems to entertain himself by taking unwarranted shots at Brady.

Despite some finding his antics mildly entertaining at first, the general consensus now is that his childish behavior is an embarrassing reflection of how low things have got for Brown, especially after everything TB did for him.

Brady has not entertained his old friend's fatuous behavior, despite the overwhelmingly crude posts. It's been rumored that Gisele might take legal action, given she has predominately featured in Brown's posts.

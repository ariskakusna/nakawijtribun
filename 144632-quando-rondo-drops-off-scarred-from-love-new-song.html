Quando Rondo Drops Off &quot;Scarred From Love&quot;

 Posted on August 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  383 words  |  Martina Birk

Quando Rondo is one of the most promising young artists emerging right now. The rapper made his name known on Youngboy Never Broke Again's 4Loyalty EP. The project put a spotlight on Quando Rondo who featured on the majority of the project. Shortly after, the rapper delivered his project Life After Fame. The rapper's been consistently dropping records for the streets. Now, the rapper returns with his latest single, "Scarred From Love."

Quando Rondo returns with a brand new record for the streets titled, "Scarred From Love." The rapper's latest single follows the release of his track "Gun Powder" which he released earlier this month. The rapper flexes his melodic delivery over this heartbreak anthem which arrives a few days after Valentine's Day. The young rapper's latest single finds him exploring love, loyalty, and infidelity as he adapts to his newfound fame.

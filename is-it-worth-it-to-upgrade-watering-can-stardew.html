When should I upgrade my Stardew watering can?

It is recommended to upgrade your watering can in Stardew Valley before a rainy day or during a season when your crops don’t need to be watered as frequently. This will ensure that your crops are well-watered and healthy before the upgrade process takes a couple of days. One ideal time to upgrade is on the 27th day of any season, as most crops don’t need to be watered on the last day and they will die when the new season comes. What is the most efficient watering Stardew?

The most efficient watering method in Stardew Valley is using sprinklers. Sprinklers automatically water your crops every day, allowing you to focus on other tasks, such as tending to animals or exploring the mines. The most efficient sprinkler in the game is the iridium sprinkler, which covers 24 tiles in a 5×5 grid. By placing multiple iridium sprinklers throughout your farm, you can minimize the time spent watering and maximize your crop yield.

Unveiling the Mystery: Division II Warriors

Drumroll please... Central Washington University competes in the glorious NCAA Division II! That's right, folks, these Wildcats are battling it out with some of the top collegiate athletes in the country, just a level below the Division I powerhouses.

Division II boasts a strong sense of community, with universities focusing on developing well-rounded student-athletes. So, while CWU might not have a mascot with a multi-million dollar shoe deal (yet!), they've got heart, hustle, and the potential to pull off some seriously impressive victories.

Bonus Fun Fact: Did you know that CWU's men's and women's rugby teams are actually Division I? Now that's something to roar about!

Why Does Division Matter?

Ah, the million-dollar question (well, maybe not a million dollars, but definitely important). Division matters because it determines the level of competition a team faces. Division I schools typically have larger budgets, more resources, and more media attention. Division II schools, on the other hand, offer a more intimate atmosphere and a strong focus on academics alongside athletics.

Remember: Division II athletes are still incredible competitors, and CWU consistently puts out impressive teams across various sports.

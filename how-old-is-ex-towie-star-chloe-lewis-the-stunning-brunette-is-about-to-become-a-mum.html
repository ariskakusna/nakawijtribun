How old is Chloe Lewis: TOWIE

Chloe Lewis is 28 years old in 2019.

She celebrates her birthday on January 5th 1991.

Chloe joined the TOWIE cast when she was 24 years old in 2015.

She’s a Capricorn which means that Chloe is meant to be a responsible and disciplined person who likes family and tradition.

Who is Chloe Lewis’ boyfriend?

Chloe was dating Jake Hall when she first appeared on The Only Way is Essex, but as everyone who watched the show saw, that relationship didn’t end well after they spent seven years together.

According to OK! Magazine, Chloe has been dating Danny Flasher since 2016.

He is said to work as a banker and the pair enjoy their life away from the limelight.

On May 22nd 2019, Chloe took to Instagram to announce that the couple are now expecting their first child!

Will Chloe make a TOWIE comeback?

It’s highly unlikely that Chloe will ever return as a cast member to ITV’s The Only Way is Essex.

She left the show after series 23 and a whole host of newbies joined the cast.

Since leaving TOWIE Chloe has had success with her own beauty brand and a swimwear collection with Boohoo.com.

On leaving the show, Chloe said in an interview with Mail Online that she wanted to focus more on her personal life.

She said: “I’m really grateful for the show, but I sort of outgrew it. I think when your relationship isn’t on the show, it’s very hard to do both…I don’t regret it and I’m thankful for all the opportunities it’s brought me.”

Air Jordan 3 &quot;Triple White&quot; Release Details Announced

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  198 words  |  Billy Koelling

In celebration of the Air Jordan 3's 30th anniversary this, Jordan Brand has released several iconic colorways and there's still much more in store in the coming months.

A "Quai 54" colorway in honor of Jordan Brand's annual basketball tournament in Paris is rumored to release sometime this Summer, as is the recently revealed "Charity Game" Air Jordan 3 which will reportedly release as part of the brand's "International Flight" collection.

If that's not enough to look forward to, a "Triple White" aka "Pure White" Air Jordan 3 is also coming this year.

As the name suggests, the kicks are built on an all-white colorway, similar to the "Pure Money" Air Jordan 3s that were unveiled in 2007. However, this upcoming pair lacks the hits of silver and “Pure $” branding, and it features a translucent blue outsole. According to reports, the kicks will be hitting retailers on July 21st for the retail price of $190.

Jordan Brand has not yet revealed official images of the all-white 3s, but early images have already surfaced giving us a really good idea of what to expect. Check out the latest images in the IG posts embedded below.

Who is BK Brasco? Net worth, store and real name of LAHH star!

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  449 words  |  Billy Koelling

BK Brasco, the Love and Hip Hop Atlanta sensation, is not just a reality star but a thriving entrepreneur. Many fans are curious about his earnings and ventures. In this article, we delve into the life of BK Brasco, his net worth in 2021, his Band of Brothers store, and the real name he goes by.

BK Brasco: The Entrepreneur

BK Brasco, originally from Brownsville, Brooklyn, is a 42-year-old entrepreneur with a diverse portfolio. Apart from his stint as a rapper, he’s famous for his role on Love and Hip Hop: Atlanta. This talented individual is also a loving father to two children from previous relationships. His journey on LAHH began in 2018 when he was introduced as Sierra Gates’ partner at the time, although they have since gone their separate ways.

BK Brasco’s Net Worth and Ventures

In 2021, BK Brasco’s estimated net worth stands at a remarkable $1.5 million. Most of this wealth was accumulated during his rap career, but he continues to earn from various sources. His musical journey commenced at the tender age of 13, and by 2013, he was gaining recognition in the music industry. One of his initial hits, “Explode,” featuring Timberland and Noelia, brought him into the limelight.

Today, BK Brasco is not only a rapper but also a successful entrepreneur. He manages clothing brands like Band of Brothers and Leonardo Romel S1, along with the hair loss solution firm, Scalp Structure Center. Among these ventures, Band of Brothers stands out as a luxurious Atlanta clothing brand with an impressive following of over 10,000 Instagram users. If you’re in Atlanta, you can visit his store at 49 Bennett St NW, Atlanta, GA 30309.

Unveiling the Real Name

While he’s known as BK Brasco to the world, his real name is Romel Cummings, given to him by his parents. Like many of his co-stars, BK Brasco adopted a stage name during his rap career. Despite this, he’s affectionately referred to as “BK” by his fellow cast members and fans alike. It’s a common practice among Love and Hip Hop Atlanta stars, with others like Yung Baby Tate also using stage names.

Conclusion

BK Brasco, the multi-talented entrepreneur, rapper, and reality star, has made a name for himself not only on Love and Hip Hop Atlanta but also in the world of business. With a net worth of $1.5 million in 2021, he’s managed to carve out a thriving career. Whether you’re a fan of his music or his appearances on TV, BK Brasco, or Romel Cummings, continues to capture hearts and attention. Keep an eye out for his ventures, particularly the popular Band of Brothers store, if you find yourself in Atlanta.

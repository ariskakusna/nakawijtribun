Kristen Stewart puts a daring spin on a classic Chanel skirt suit

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  220 words  |  Billy Koelling

Kristen Stewart – who is currently promoting her new movie Seberg – took to the red carpet last night in a pink Chanel skirt suit, but gave the ensemble a daring twist, choosing to go bare underneath.

At a Los Angeles screening of the film, Stewart posed for pictures wearing a light-pink taffeta pleated skirt with a matching jacket, which featured an embellished trim.

Although the two-piece was shown on the catwalk with a striped T-shirt, the actress chose not to wear a top underneath, drawing attention to her statement necklace. She added thick black tights and flat shoes to the look, creating a perfect balance of daring and demure.

Victor VIRGILE//Getty Images

Stewart's look is the latest A-list moment to suggest that naked dressing might be about to make a return to the red carpet. Over the weekend, supermodel Kendall Jenner reinvigorated the trend in a festive way, wearing a nude David Koma dress with a tinsel trim.

The last few years have seen stars opt for a more covered-up approach to red-carpet dressing after the naked trend reached new heights in 2017, but it looks like that might all be about to change as we head into the new decade.

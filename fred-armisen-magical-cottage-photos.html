'Wednesday' Star Fred Armisen Buys Magical $4 Million Cottage: Photos

 Posted on August 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  418 words  |  Tandra Barner

Did you really expect Fred Armisen, one of the stars of Wednesday and an SNL alum, to have anything other than a unique love of old-world architecture? He and his partner and Wednesday co-star Riki Lindhome just dropped a little over $4 million on a four-bedroom, three-and-a-half-bathroom Los Feliz home that looks like it came to life from a Disney movie!

Related story Fred Armisen finally confirms he's leaving Saturday Night Live

Along with an eccentric floorplan, the cottage has a peculiar history behind it, originally being built for a woman in the 1920s. Then a notable woman named Leona P. Wood, who was an artist and the widow of a wealthy businessman, stayed in the home. Then Dr. Paul DeGaston lived in the home for a while, and if that name sounds familiar to you, it’s because he was a decades-long suspect in the Black Dahlia murder case. Then it was sold multiple more times, most notably to Grey’s Anatomy star T.R. Knight, producer Lauren Lexton, and now Armisen and Lindhome.

Not only is there a rather odd history behind it, but there are so many beautiful details around the cottage; from the high ceilings to the stunning moldings. Seriously, this is something out of a cottagecore lover’s dream!

Fall in love with this home with us while we check out the photos of the California abode, and make sure to check out more photos and details at Dirt!

  • Fairytale-esque Gardens

    These lush gardens are perfect for a fantasy film, especially since we’re getting fairy vibes from all the ancient trees and overgrown backyard. 

  • Fully Updated Kitchen

    Now, this kitchen may seem a bit different than the rest of the home, but it’s just as magical. Not only is the color palette stunning, but this updated kitchen has Wolf, Sub-Zero, and Bertazzoni appliances.

  • Atypical Floorplan

    Now, this home is said to have an atypical floorplan, with the main floor having high-ceilinged rooms like the living and dining rooms, kitchen, and den.

  • Amenities Everywhere

    Along with two sprawling guest bedrooms, there are a bunch of sitting rooms, an office, movie theater, and a two-car garage, to name a few. 

  • Gorgeous Detailing Like No Other

    Remember those amazing details we were talking about? There are diamond-paned windows, enchanting woodwork throughout, and original hand-carved doors.

US rapper Nas has opened up about what he thinks about the developments in hip-hop. Nas told the Financial Times in an interview that he appreciates all the artists that are on the come up but they aren't competition for him.

“I appreciate what’s out there, but there’s no one keeping me up at night," he said.

Nas said that he'll listen to a new rap song that comes up but nothing keeps him entertained enough to find him listening to it the next week.

The Queens rapper said that although he's not really bothered about the new artists in the game, the late Pop Smoke caught his eye.

What exactly is Gfebs?

A web-based enterprise resource planning system (ERP) for the United States Army, GFEBS (General Fund Enterprise Business System) is also known as GFEBS. In order to unify business processes and transactional input throughout the Army, GFEBS is replacing or absorbing more than 80 outdated accounting and asset management systems, according to the Army.

What exactly is the Gfebs mandate and what are the requirements?

According to the specifications, GFEBS was required to: • Provide a modern finance capacity to support the modular, joint, and expeditionary armies. Support the timely, dependable, and accurate collection and presentation of financial data for decision-making. Replace any old systems or procedures that are preventing dependable execution from taking place. Comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

Kid brutally tackled to the ground after jumping onto the pitch at Buccaneers-Falcons game

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  135 words  |  Patria Henriques

There have been criticisms directed towards Tampa security guards after they violently tackled a young boy who attempted to run onto the pitch during the team's recent game against the Atlanta Falcons.

The incident, which took place in the second quarter of the match, lasted just a few seconds before security guards tackled the boy to the ground. He was then forcefully held down before being escorted out of the stadium.

The Tampa Police Department has since reported that the case will go no further than just a smack on the wrist. No arrest was made.

The boy's mother was vocal about her disapproval of the treatment of her "10-year-old son".

However, many people on social media were critical of the boy's mother and questioned why a parent would let her child do that.

Whats the benefit of making a skill legendary in Skyrim?

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  724 words  |  Billy Koelling

Making a skill legendary in Skyrim has several benefits. When a skill is made legendary, it resets to level 15 and refunds all perk points spent in that skill tree. This allows the player to reallocate perk points to other skills and choose a different path of progression. Additionally, making a skill legendary increases the number of perks that can affect the player over time, as perk points can be earned again by leveling up the skill. This can make the character more powerful and versatile in the long run.

Should I make my skill legendary in Skyrim?

The main benefit of making a skill legendary in Skyrim is that it allows you to keep leveling your character without having to branch out into skills you don’t want to use. So, a stealth-based character can level up exclusively on Sneak and Pickpocket and the like without ever having to touch heavy armor or destruction.

What happens if I make my perks legendary in Skyrim?

Once a skill has reached 100, it can be made “Legendary.” This resets the skill back to level 15 and refunds any Perk points that were spent in that skill tree. It allows you to continue leveling up and earning perk points, increasing the number of perks affecting your character over time.

What happens if you make a skill legendary twice in Skyrim?

Making a skill legendary twice in Skyrim is possible. You can reach level 100, reset the skill to level 15, level it up to 100 again, and then have the option to make it legendary once more. This allows you to gain additional perks and level up your character even further.

Do legendary skills level faster?

No, legendary skills do not level faster than regular skills. The rate at which skills level up remains the same regardless of whether they are legendary or not.

Can you go past 100 in a skill Skyrim?

No, once a skill reaches level 100 in Skyrim, it cannot go any higher. However, you can reset the skill back to level 15 and level it up again to gain additional perk points and improve your character’s abilities.

What comes after legendary in Skyrim?

There is no status past Legendary in Skyrim. Once a skill has been made legendary, you can continue to level it up, gain skill experience, and earn perk points to further enhance your character’s abilities.

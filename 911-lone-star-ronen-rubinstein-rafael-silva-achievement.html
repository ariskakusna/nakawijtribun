9-1-1: Lone Star's Ronen Rubinstein and Rafael Silva celebrate incredible achievement

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  380 words  |  Tandra Barner

9-1-1 Lone Star was named Best Drama Series at the 2023 GLAAD Media Awards

    9-1-1 Lone Star was named Best Drama Series at the 2023 GLAAD Media Awards on Thursday March 30, and the show's leading LGBTQ+ men were on hand to accept the award and give a beautiful tribute to their peers. 

    "WE WON OUTSTANDING DRAMA SERIES AT THE 34TH ANNUAL #GLAADAWARDS ! Thank you @GLAAD ! This was truly a surreal moment, one that I will cherish forever," wrote Ronen Rubinstein, who stars as paramedic TK Strand. 

    Sarah Michelle Gellar handed the trio their award, and Brian Michael Smith gave a speech that left many in tears as he spoke of the very real and political threats that are facing the gay and trans communities. 

    "Moments like these are a light in dark times for our community," said Brian, who stars as firefighter Paul Strickland in the Fox drama. Brian came out as a trans man in 2017 after acting for many years. 

    "In a deep field of beautiful, authentic and impact shows, @911lonestar won Outstanding Drama Series at last night’s @glaad Media Awards. I’m incredibly grateful to GLAAD not only for the recognition of our show, but for the work they do to ensure the representation of LGBTQ+ people in media is fair, authentic and inclusive," he wrote on Instagram alongside a picture of him and co-stars Ronen Rubinstein and Rafael Silva.
